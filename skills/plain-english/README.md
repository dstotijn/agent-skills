# plain-english

A skill that helps you write and edit human-facing prose so it reads clearly,
concisely, and directly.

## Attribution

The rules and examples in this skill are adapted from **"How to write in plain
English"** by the [Plain English Campaign](https://www.plainenglish.co.uk),
one of the free guides published at
<https://www.plainenglish.co.uk/free-guides>.

The Plain English Campaign owns the copyright to the original guide. This skill
distills its guidance for use by AI coding agents; it is not affiliated with or
endorsed by the Plain English Campaign.

## Contents

- `SKILL.md` - the rules, the "don't over-correct" guardrail, and the
  completion check.
- `references/words-to-avoid.md` - bureaucratic words and their plainer swaps.
- `references/apologising.md` - how to word a reply to a complaint or a
  difficult problem.
