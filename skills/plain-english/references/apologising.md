# Apologising

When replying to a tricky letter, a complaint, or a difficult problem, put yourself in the reader's shoes. Be professional, not emotional.

- If you are going to apologise, do so early.
- If the problem is your fault, say so. Apologise completely and concisely, sympathetically and sincerely.
- You may have to give a firm, unwelcome answer. Still be as helpful and polite as you can.
- Whether or not it is your fault, emphasise what you can do for the other person.
